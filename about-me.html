About Me

My story starts with a simple “Hello, World”. A program I wrote in Python that thanks to that line of code, drove me to develop a passion for programming in general. Along the way I built many small programs in python, but what really drove me into web development was a blog-style application that I had built with Django, a web framework . This project was the turning point of my life that led me to where I am today, a passionate web developer who loves creating things on the web for people to see and admire. I also develop a strong passion for learning and picking up new skills that I think will make me a better software engineer.
