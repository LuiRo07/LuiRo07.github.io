About Luis Rosas

My story starts with a simple “Hello, World”. A program I wrote in Python, a programming language that drove me to develop a passion for programming in general. Along the way I built many small programs in python, but what really drove me into web development was the way you can bring your ideas to life and display them for the world to see.

With the combination of technical skills and creativity, and an endless spiral of imagination, you can create those ideas from scratch. When I'm not writing code for the web, I invest my free time in practicing and learning to play my guitar. I also like to go hiking and enjoy beautiful sceneries of the city below. To me, a website is more than just a site, it is an extension of creativity skills that can help express an idea or message globally through the internet.

I am a front-end Developer with design skills to bring any creative idea to the web.
